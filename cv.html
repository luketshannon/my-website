Bio

Luke Shannon (b. 2000) is a contemporary artist using code and art machines, examining selfhood in systems. He is currently a Steve Jobs Archive Fellow in NYC, and has exhibited or performed internationally with ARTXCODE (NYC), The Digital (Art Basel Miami), Tonic (SF, NYC), Proof+Pace Gallery (London), Bright Moments (LA, CDMX, Paris, Venice, Berlin), Art Blocks (Marfa, TX), Princeton University (NJ), and Dia Beacon (NY). Luke is online at lukeshannon.xyz.
